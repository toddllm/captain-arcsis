# Captain Arcsis - ENHANCED EDITION

**An epic 8-bit 2D dungeon adventure featuring 4 playable character variants, infinite equipment evolution, progressive boss battles, and endless replayability!**

## NEW IN ENHANCED EDITION

### Captain Arcsis Color Variants
Choose your hero! Each variant has unique abilities and playstyles:

- **YELLOW - Magic Master**: Enhanced spell power (+50%), faster mana regen (2x), glowing magic eyes
- **RED - Attack Destroyer**: Devastating attack power (+80%), high crit chance (+15%), flame patterns
- **GREEN - Defense Tank**: Double defense, HP regeneration, nature-infused armor
- **BLUE - Speed Demon**: Lightning fast movement (+50%), dodge chance (+20%), speed lines

### INFINITE Equipment Evolution
- **Swords**: Level 1 to INFINITY! Evolve through combat and shop purchases
  - Common → Uncommon → Rare → Epic → Legendary → Mythic → DIVINE
  - Visual changes: Blade grows longer, glows brighter, adds runes
  - Damage scales exponentially with level

- **Shields**: No level cap! Evolve through blocking damage
  - Each level increases defense and size
  - Rarity tiers affect damage reduction multiplier
  - Divine shields have special symbols

### ANIZON - Progressive Recurring Boss
**Anizon appears at the END OF EVERY DUNGEON LEVEL, getting harder each time!**

- **Encounter 1**: 15,000 HP, 200 ATK, 100 DEF
- **Encounter 2**: 30,000 HP, 250 ATK, 125 DEF + Meteor Storm
- **Encounter 3**: 56,250 HP, 300 ATK, 150 DEF + Time Freeze
- **Encounter 4+**: GOD-LIKE power scaling!

**Anizon's New Features:**
- **Summons Friends**: Shadow Warrior, Ghost Knight, Dark Mage, Void Walker, Death Knight, and more!
- **Drops Secret Keys**: Use them to unlock hidden doors and secrets
- **Unlocks Companions**: Defeat Anizon to add his friends to your party
- **Dynamic Phases**: More phases unlock with each encounter
- **New Attacks**: Meteor Storm, Time Freeze, Dimension Collapse, Ultimate Destruction

### Shop System (Press B)
Buy equipment upgrades with coins earned from enemies:
- **Upgrade Sword**: Instantly increase sword level (exponential pricing)
- **Upgrade Shield**: Boost your defense
- **Health Potions**: 50% HP restore (use with 1 key)
- **Mana Potions**: 50% mana restore (use with 2 key)
- **Mega Potions**: FULL HP + Mana + Clear status effects (use with 3 key)
- **Learn Spells**: Unlock new fairy magic abilities
- **Secret Keys**: For Anizon's locked doors

### Skill Tree System (Press K)
Spend skill points (earned every 5 levels) on permanent upgrades:

**Combat Skills:**
- Sword Mastery: +10% sword damage per level
- Shield Mastery: +10% shield effectiveness per level
- Critical Strike: +5% crit chance per level
- Combo Master: +2s combo window per level
- Lifesteal: 5% HP steal per level

**Magic Skills:**
- Mana Pool: +20 max mana per level
- Spell Power: +15% spell damage per level
- Fast Casting: -10% cooldown per level
- Mana Regen: +50% mana regeneration per level

**Survival Skills:**
- Vitality: +25 max HP per level
- Regeneration: +1 HP/second per level
- Dodge Roll: +3% dodge chance per level
- Last Stand: 10% chance to survive fatal hit per level

### Combo System
- Chain hits to build combos
- Combo multiplier increases damage
- Visual combo counter (3x, 5x, 10x+)
- Bonus EXP for high combos (10+ hits)
- Combo timer extends with Combo Master skill

### Critical Hit System
- Base 5% crit chance
- Red Arcsis: +15% crit chance
- Critical Strike skill: +5% per level
- Critical hits deal 2.5x damage
- Golden slash effects and sparkles

### Status Effects
Enemies and bosses can inflict:
- **Poison**: 2 damage/second for 5s (green overlay)
- **Burn**: 3 damage/second for 4s (orange overlay)
- **Freeze**: 50% slow for 3s (cyan overlay)
- **Stun**: No movement for 1.5s (yellow overlay)
- **Bleed**: 4 damage/second for 6s (dark red overlay)
- **Weakness**: -30% attack for 8s (gray overlay)
- **Curse**: -50% defense for 10s (purple overlay)

### Spell Learning
Start with Light spell, learn more through:
- Shop purchases
- Progress through combat (auto-learn at 100%)
- Available spells: Heal, Blast, Shield, Fireball, Icestorm, Lightning, Earthquake, Teleport, Timestop, Meteor

### Achievement Tracking
- Level milestones (10, 50, 100)
- Total damage dealt/taken
- Enemies killed counter
- Bosses defeated tracker
- Secrets found counter
- Max combo achieved

### Infinite Dungeon Generation
- Procedurally generated dungeon levels
- Scaling enemy difficulty
- More chests with better rewards
- Secret doors requiring Anizon's keys
- No end! Keep defeating Anizon forever!

## Controls

### Movement & Combat
- **WASD / Arrow Keys** - Move Arcsis
- **SPACE** - Attack with sword
- **E** - Interact (buttons, doors, chests)
- **F** - Use Fairy Magic

### Menus & Management
- **B** - Open Shop (during gameplay)
- **K** - Open Skill Tree (during gameplay)
- **I** - Open Inventory/Stats
- **ESC** - Pause Menu

### Potions (Quick Use)
- **1** - Use Health Potion
- **2** - Use Mana Potion
- **3** - Use Mega Potion

## Story

Deep in the enchanted forest, young Captain Arcsis discovers a glowing mysterious button on a stone pedestal. Curious, he presses it and is transported to a dark dungeon ruled by the terrifying ANIZON - The University Destroyer. With the help of a magical fairy companion, Arcsis must:

- Choose his path (Yellow, Red, Green, or Blue)
- Fight increasingly tough enemies
- Level up his equipment to INFINITE power
- Defeat Anizon REPEATEDLY as he grows stronger
- Unlock Anizon's friends and secrets
- Become the ultimate warrior!

## Technical Details

- **Engine**: HTML5 Canvas + Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+)
- **Graphics**: 8-bit pixel art (procedurally drawn)
- **Audio**: Web Audio API (retro synthesized sounds)
- **Storage**: LocalStorage (auto-save every 60s)
- **Size**: ~7,000 lines of JavaScript
- **Dependencies**: NONE! Pure browser game
- **Performance**: Targets 60 FPS with efficient rendering

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in a modern web browser
2. Select "New Game" from the menu
3. **Choose your Arcsis color variant!**
4. Watch the story unfold in the enchanted forest
5. Press the mysterious button (E key) to enter the dungeon
6. Fight monsters to earn coins and experience
7. Visit the Shop (B key) to upgrade equipment
8. Spend skill points (K key) when available
9. Use potions (1, 2, 3 keys) in tough battles
10. Defeat Anizon at the end of each level
11. **Keep going! Anizon returns stronger each time!**

## Pro Tips

1. **Red Arcsis** is best for speedruns (high damage)
2. **Green Arcsis** is best for survival (HP regen + defense)
3. **Yellow Arcsis** is best for magic builds (spell power)
4. **Blue Arcsis** is best for dodge builds (avoid damage)
5. Buy potions BEFORE fighting Anizon
6. Max out Vitality skill first for survival
7. Critical Strike + Red Arcsis = MASSIVE damage
8. Secret keys open doors with AMAZING rewards
9. High combos give bonus EXP - chain those hits!
10. Last Stand skill can save you at 0 HP!
