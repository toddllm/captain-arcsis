# Captain Arcsis

An 8-bit 2D dungeon adventure game where a young boy named Arcsis becomes trapped in a mysterious dungeon after pressing a cursed button in the enchanted forest.

## Story

Deep in the enchanted forest, young Arcsis discovers a glowing mysterious button on a stone pedestal. Curious, he presses it and is transported to a dark dungeon filled with dangerous monsters and puzzles. With the help of a magical fairy companion, Arcsis must:

- Fight tough enemies (even the weakest are dangerous!)
- Solve puzzles and collect coins
- Level up his sword and evolve his shield
- Defeat the mighty bosses
- Escape the dungeon and save the day!

## Features

### Combat System
- **Extreme battle mechanics** - Every enemy is tough, even Dark Slimes!
- **Sword leveling** - Your weapon grows stronger through combat (up to Level 10)
- **Shield evolution** - Block damage and evolve your defense
- **No power-ups** - Pure skill and strategy like classic games
- **No jumping** - Strategic ground-based combat

### Enemies
- **Skeleton Knights** - Heavy armor, devastating attacks (150 HP!)
- **Zombies** - Regenerate health over time, relentless (120 HP)
- **Dark Slimes** - Fast and dangerous (80 HP)
- **Ghost Warriors** - Can phase through attacks (100 HP)
- **Dungeon Bats** - Quick and annoying (60 HP)
- **Armored Golems** - Tank enemies with massive defense (250 HP)

### Boss Battles

#### Origami Mirda - Lightning Goddess
- A deity reborn in paper form
- Lightning strikes and paper storms
- Creates origami clones
- Thunder crashes that shake the dungeon

#### ANIZON - The University Destroyer (FINAL BOSS)
- **THE STRONGEST AND MOST POWERFUL BOSS IN THE GAME!**
- Robot/human-like creature
- Even the strongest armor and swords barely scratch him!
- **Teleportation** - Appears anywhere instantly
- **Summons Monster Horsemen** - Surrounds himself with ghost warriors
- **Insane attacks:**
  - Devastation Beam
  - Void Crush
  - Reality Tear
  - Death Spiral
- **Three phases** of increasing power
- 5000 HP with massive defense

### Companion
- **Fairy** - A magical companion from the enchanted forest
  - Heal magic to restore HP
  - Protective shield for temporary invincibility
  - Magic blast to damage all nearby enemies
  - Light magic to illuminate dark areas
  - Helpful dialogue and hints

### Game Features
- **Dialogue system** with story progression
- **Puzzle mechanics** - Switches, levers, keys, and doors
- **Coin collection** for scoring
- **Save/Load system** - Progress saves automatically
- **Multiple dungeon levels** with increasing difficulty
- **Experience and leveling** system

## Controls

- **WASD / Arrow Keys** - Move Arcsis
- **SPACE** - Attack with sword
- **E** - Interact (buttons, doors, chests)
- **F** - Use Fairy Magic
- **I** - Open Inventory
- **ESC** - Pause Menu

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in a modern web browser
2. Select "New Game" from the menu
3. Watch the story unfold as Arcsis explores the forest
4. Press the mysterious button when prompted
5. Fight monsters, solve puzzles, and collect coins
6. Level up your equipment through combat
7. Defeat the bosses to escape the dungeon!

## Technical Details

- Built with HTML5 Canvas and vanilla JavaScript
- 8-bit pixel art graphics (no external assets needed)
- Web Audio API for retro sound effects
- LocalStorage for save/load functionality
- No dependencies required
